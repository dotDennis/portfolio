Changes

Most time was spent creating this website, and I've implemented the H1 SEO improvement here.

Text width limited

About page & blog post specific pages have their text max-width limited to 700px & 900px (Order left to right: About page, blog post specific pages). This was supposed to be in the main hand-in but apperantly I never added it - and became blind of my own work. Imporant to have people review your websites, both early and late, noted!

Icons margin

The social icons have a beautiful nicely aligned margin, this was included in the original hand-in... But, I never added it! (Atleast for desktop, mobile had it). I therefor added a small 5px margin left/right on my exam project. It's not much, but it's just enough to make the icons breathe (more).

Alt text

While making my blog feed, I used "alt text" as a placeholder, until the API was properly setup. This placeholder was never removed. Until now, the blog feed is now 100% (minus a few percentages) accessible! (Great job Dennis! - Thank you!)
